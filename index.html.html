Testing Protocol

Four-stage verification.
Zero compromise.

Every Vericor compound passes through a sequential, multi-stage testing protocol before a single unit is approved for release.

Stage 01

Identity Confirmation

HPLC and mass spectrometry confirm exact peptide sequence and molecular identity against specification.

Stage 02

Purity Analysis

Reverse-phase HPLC determines precise purity levels, flagging any impurities above the established threshold.

Stage 03

Stability Testing

Accelerated and real-time stability studies confirm shelf-life, storage requirements, and degradation profiles.

Stage 04

Third-Party Audit

Independent laboratory confirmation of all results before any batch receives final release approval.
